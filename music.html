Music

Ty Maxwell is a musician, songwriter and writer currently based in the United States.

An in-demand session musician, Ty has worked with a wide variety of artists live and on record, such as The World Is A Beautiful Place & I Am No Longer Afraid To Die, Strange Ranger, Another Michael, Pinegrove, Fenne Lily, Sinai Vessel, Emperor X, Shannen Moser, Sam & Louise, and Lindsay Reamer. He writes, releases and performs music under the moniker Thank You Thank You.

In the dog days of summer '24, TYTY released the single "Watching The Cyclones" on Glamour Gowns, which Stereogum's Danielle Chelosky called "an abundant, ripe song…that feels like soaking in sunlight"; this past winter, Ruination Records released the post-classical compositions "After You Gave Me This" and "There's No Telling", of which Chris Deville wrote, "there's a lot going on here, and it's all gorgeous." More new music is on the way.
